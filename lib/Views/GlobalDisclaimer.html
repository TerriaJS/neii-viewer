The National Environmental Information Infrastructure (NEII) service is being decommissioned on 15 June 2022.

Environmental information will continue to be made available through Australian Government services such as data.gov.au, NationalMap and Research Vocabularies Australia. For more details on accessing the environmental information, please go to the NEII data transition information page. If you require assistance in locating specific datasets, or have any questions, please email environment@bom.gov.au.

Thank you for your interest in, and support of, NEII.
